Joan's BBQ Chicken Wings (aka Chicken Bones)

Ingredients

  • 24 Chicken Wings
  • 1/2 cup Honey
  • 2 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/3 cup Soy Sauce
  • 2 lemons (juice only)
  • 1 garlic clove

Directions

  1. Discard wing tips and break wings in two pieces if necessary.
  2. Line shallow pan with foil (very messy to clean up otherwise).
  3. Mix all ingredients and pour over wings.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour. Baste every 15 minutes.
