Treasure Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 - 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cut butter, softened
  • 1 1/3 cup flaked coconut
  • 1 - 12 oz. pkg. semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. In small bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, flour and baking powder.
  3. In large mixer bowl, beat sweetened condensed milk and butter until smooth.
  4. Add graham cracker crumb mixture; mix well.
  5. Stir in coconut, chocolate chips and walnuts.
  6. Drop by 2 teaspoon scoop onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake 9 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned.
  8. Store loosely covered at room temperature.
